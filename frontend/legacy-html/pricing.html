§03 / FAQ PRICING · BILLING · LEGAL
FAQ

Pricing,
in plain English.

Still have questions? Talk to a human.

Is there really a free tier?
Yes. Forever. For individual candidates and 1-job employers, the platform is free with no card on file. The free tier doesn't time out into a paywall — it just stays free.
How does per-seat billing work?
Each seat is one recruiter or hiring-manager login. Read-only viewers (HR ops, panel interviewers, executives) are always free. You can add or remove seats any month.
What counts as an AI interview?
One completed AI mock or screening session per candidate per role. Re-tries by the same candidate within 7 days don't recount. Failed sessions (under 5 min) don't count.
Do colleges pay per student?
No — colleges are billed per cohort, annually. One flat fee covers every student, every interview, every report. We don't believe in per-seat pricing for students.
Can I switch plans mid-cycle?
Upgrades pro-rate immediately. Downgrades take effect at the start of the next billing period. We never auto-upgrade you for going over a limit — you'll get a notification first.
What happens to my data if I leave?
You can export everything — applicants, interviews, recordings, reports — as JSON, CSV, and MP4 at any time. Cancellation deletes your data within 30 days. No retention games.
Do you offer discounts for nonprofits or universities?
Yes. Registered 501(c)(3) and government educational institutions get 40% off any plan. Email us a copy of your registration and we'll set it up the same day.
