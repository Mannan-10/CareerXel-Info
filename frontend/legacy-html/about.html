MANIFESTO

Hiring is broken.
We're building the better version.

We're building a platform where the candidate is heard, the recruiter has receipts, and the college has the numbers it can actually defend.

§01 / JOURNEY 2024 → 2026
JOURNEY

Every milestone,
in the order it happened.

2024

The first version

CareerXel started as a side project: an AI mock-interview tool for the founder's cousin's placement prep. By month three, it had 800 weekly users and zero spend on marketing.

▢ FOUNDED · MAR 2024 · KOTA
2024

First college partnership

A regional engineering college signed up to onboard their entire 2025 batch. We built bulk CSV onboarding in 11 days. They placed 68% of their cohort — up from 41% the year before.

▢ JUL 2024 · 412 STUDENTS · 1 CAMPUS
2025

Seed round & recruiter ATS

$4.2M seed led by Lightspeed and Blume. We launched the recruiter ATS — stages, scorecards, calendars — and added our first 50 employer partners. SOC 2 Type I shipped in the same quarter.

▢ FEB 2025 · $4.2M · LIGHTSPEED + BLUME
2025

Proctoring & the scoring engine

Identity verification, face presence, and the 12-dimension scoring engine — all calibrated against real outcomes from our partner cohorts. SOC 2 Type II by year-end.

▢ NOV 2025 · 12 DIMS · SOC 2 TYPE II
2026

Series A & the international launch

$22M Series A led by Sequoia. Offices in Bangalore and SF. First international cohorts onboarded from Singapore and Dubai. The team grew to 47.

▢ APR 2026 · $22M · SEQUOIA
Now

What we're building next

Private fine-tuned models per enterprise. Video-first interview replay. Career roadmaps tied directly into LMS. The list is long. We're hiring.
